About Us

Our Navratri Committee, Chhatra Dal, has been organizing cultural and devotional events for decades. We aim to bring the community together through Bhajans and festive celebrations of Maa Durga.

Durga Puja has been celebrated here since the 1960s, with traditional rituals like Murti Sthapna on Saptami, on Navami Kanya Pujan, a vibrant Mela, Bhandara (Maha Prasad), and soulful folk songs (Geet). From boys and men to elders, all community members actively contribute, making this celebration a true collective spirit.

The festivities conclude with Visarjan, where the entire community joins together in devotion and unity.

Address: Purab Muhalla Braham Sthan, Shri Hanuman Mandir, Mirganj, Gopalganj, Bihar.
